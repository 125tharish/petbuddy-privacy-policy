Privacy Policy for Pet Buddy

Your privacy is important to us. Pet Buddy is designed to protect user and pet information and does not collect or store personal data beyond what is necessary for core functionality.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number during registration.
  • Pet Information: Pet profiles, photos, breed details, and medical records provided by users.
  • Location Data: Location information used only for lost pet alerts and nearby notifications.
  • Usage Data: Basic, anonymized app usage data to improve performance and user experience.

How We Use Data

Data is used solely to support core app features such as lost pet recovery, AI photo matching, real-time alerts, and pet care management. Pet Buddy does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Data Security

All user credentials and sensitive data are stored using encrypted and hashed formats. Secure APIs and role-based access controls are implemented to prevent unauthorized access.

Data Deletion
